About Me

The short version of a long story about curiosity, terminals, and cloud bills.

I'm a Software Engineering graduate from Pokhara University with a full-stack foundation and a clear direction: cloud infrastructure, DevOps, and security engineering.

I started with the MERN stack, shipping real products end to end. Along the way I discovered what I enjoy most isn't just building features — it's designing the systems underneath them: networks, pipelines, deployments, and the security model that keeps it all safe.

Right now I'm preparing for the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, containerizing everything I touch, and automating the boring parts of my day. Off the keyboard: early gym sessions, skincare science, and over-engineering my personal projects on purpose.
